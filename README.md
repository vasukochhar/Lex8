# LEX8: The Defensible Legal AI Platform ⚖️🛡️

**Every citation verified. Every action governed. Every output you would file in court.**

Lex8 is a collaborative AI workspace for lawyers (drafting, research, tabular review, workflow automation) built directly on top of **Anchor8**—a multi-modal governance fabric that intercepts every action by every agent *before* it executes and stamps a cryptographically signed defensibility certificate on the output.

---

## 🛑 The Problem

Every law firm with a partner-level signature on the line faces an unsolved problem: AI tools produce great work, but no tool produces work the firm can confidently file in court without manual verification of every citation, fact, privileged passage, and potential bias risk. 

With **EU AI Act Article 14** classifying Legal AI as a high-risk system requiring traceable audit logs and effective human oversight, the legal exposure of using unchecked AI is now structural. 

## 🚀 The Vision

Lex8 is the first legal AI platform where the answer to *"can you prove this output is defensible?"* is a cryptographically signed **yes**—generated automatically, on every action, by Anchor8. 

Every action a Lex8 agent takes is intercepted before execution:
1. **Lane 1:** Schema-validated.
2. **Lane 2:** Scored by a 12-detector Adaptive Engine (~200ms).
3. **Lane 3:** Escalated to the dual-juror Tribunal if the score lands in the uncertainty band (~3.5s).
4. **Lane 4:** Routed to attorney review for irreversible high-stakes calls. 

Every verdict produces an EU AI Act Article 14-compliant forensic narrative, signed with AWS KMS keys held by the firm's Anchor8 tenant.

## 🧠 The 8 Archetypes (Modules)

Every meaningful piece of legal work touches eight distinct agent types. Lex8 maps each archetype to a named product module:

1. **Drafter (LLM Agent):** Claude Opus / DeepSeek integration for brief drafting with inline hallucination checks and structural rule engines.
2. **Filer (RPA Agent):** Playwright PACER mock flow with DOM capture and pre-submit attorney HMAC auth guards.
3. **Vault Vision (Vision Agent):** Tesseract OCR pipeline detecting redaction leaks by diffing text-layers vs. visible-layers.
4. **Library (Retrieval / Memory Agent):** Hybrid BM25 + Voyage dense vector search connected to CourtListener data.
5. **Forecast (ML-Statistical Agent):** XGBoost + LightGBM ensemble for outcome prediction with TreeSHAP attribution and bias detection.
6. **War Room (Swarm Agent):** CrewAI orchestrator for multi-agent redlining and parallel workflow processing.
7. **Validator (Neuro-Symbolic Agent):** Souffle Datalog rules engine resolving logical compliance conflicts.
8. **Case Synth (Multi-Modal Agent):** Timeline builder fusing docs, video, audio, and photos with Whisper transcription and contradiction detection.
